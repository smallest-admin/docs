## What is Sapien?

Sapien is an AI agent that can do phone calls for you. Typically, people use Sapien for lead-qualification
and lead retargetting but you can also use it for customer support, surveys and informational calls.

## What are some of our key features?

Sapien has the lowest latency in the world for a voice AI agent (~500ms). We self-host our LLMs so your data never leaves our servers.
Sapien is highly customizable and with simple prompts, you can configure Sapien to sell any product to any customer.
